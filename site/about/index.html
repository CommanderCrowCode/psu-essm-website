About the Program

An international doctoral program producing independent researchers who integrate knowledge across disciplines to solve natural-resource and environmental challenges.

Why this program

Natural disasters are more frequent and more intense, causing major economic, environmental, and social losses. Understanding and solving these problems requires knowledge integrated across many disciplines. This program exists to produce graduates who can face these challenges and become part of the solution — creating new knowledge and integrating fields to prevent and solve natural-disaster problems and manage natural resources and the environment.

Who it's for

Researchers and professionals who want to lead in environmental science and sustainability — in academia, government, industry, or NGOs.

Faculty of Technology and Environment

Established in 2005, the FTE produces outstanding graduates and research in technology and the environment, applying knowledge to concrete projects for local and national needs, with emphasis on managing natural resources and the environment in the Andaman coast region.

PSU Phuket Campus

Prince of Songkla University, founded in 1967, is the first university in southern Thailand. The Phuket Campus (a full campus since 1993) serves the southern Andaman region with an emphasis on international-caliber education, research, and regional development. Campus vision: "An International Education Center with Global Sustainable Development."
