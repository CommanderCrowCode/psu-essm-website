Admissions

We welcome applicants ready to undertake original, internationally relevant research in environmental science and sustainability.

Eligibility

Plan 1.1 — a master's degree (or equivalent) in environmental science, environmental management, environmental engineering, or a related field, plus evidence of English proficiency (below).

Plan 1.2 — a bachelor's degree (or equivalent) with very good academic performance in those fields, with sufficient knowledge, ability, and potential to undertake a thesis, plus evidence of English proficiency.

Other qualifications follow the PSU Graduate Studies Regulations B.E. 2563.
