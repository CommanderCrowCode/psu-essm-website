# Image Prompts — ESSM Website (for operator to generate)

Per operator instruction (2026-06-22): images that **can** be AI-generated are left as
placeholders in the site (duotone photo wells / gradient hero), with generation prompts
collected here. Generate when ready, drop files into `site/assets/img/`, and swap the
placeholder for an `<img>` (keep a subtle duotone/overlay so blues stay dominant — brand guide).

## ⚠️ Truthfulness boundary — what NOT to AI-generate

AI imagery is fine for **atmospheric / illustrative** scenes. It must **NOT** be used for
anything that asserts a verifiable fact:

- ❌ **Faculty / people portraits** — must be real, consented photos (brand guide: "No fake
  faculty," "No stock-photo guesses for faculty"). Leave as real-photo-only placeholders.
- ❌ **Specific real PSU Phuket campus buildings / labs / field stations** — a generated
  "campus" would misrepresent the actual place. Use real photos when available.
- ✅ OK: Andaman coastline, sea, reefs, mangroves, coastal landscapes, abstract
  science/sustainability visuals — clearly evocative, not documentary.

## Shared style preamble (prepend to every prompt)

> Editorial, cinematic, high-end university brand photography style. **Duotone color
> grade** in the PSU ESSM palette: deep indigo-purple `#2C2A6E` → faculty purple `#353583`
> in shadows, lifted with bright river-blue `#59CBE8` highlights; restrained and
> authoritative, not saturated or "stock-y". Soft natural light, generous negative space,
> no text, no logos, no watermarks, no identifiable faces. Subtle fine-line texture
> acceptable. Blues/indigos dominant.
