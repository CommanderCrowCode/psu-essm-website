## Graduation criteria (TQF2 หมวด 7) — VERIFIED (Plans 1.1 & 1.2)

1. Pass the **Qualifying Examination** (to become eligible to do the thesis).
2. Submit thesis + **pass the final open oral defense** (committee of internal + external
   examiners; public/open session); achieving graduate-level learning outcomes.
3. **Publication:** thesis (or part) **published or accepted in quality international journals
   — at least 2 articles**; OR
4. at least **1** international journal article **+** at least 1 innovation/creative work with
   commercial/social/economic use, OR at least 1 patent.
7. Pass the **foreign-language requirement** per the Graduate School announcement.
Grading + other criteria per **PSU Graduate Studies Regulations B.E. 2563**.
(NOTE: no numeric GPA threshold stated in TQF2 — do not publish one.)
